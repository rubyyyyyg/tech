Riverside Tech Conference

Innovate, Connect, Elevate: Shaping the Future Together

An annual conference that brings together industry leaders and enthusiasts to explore the latest trends and innovations in technology through keynotes, workshops, and discussions.

Dr. Emily Johnson – Chief AI Scientist at InnovateTech

Dr. Emily Johnson, an AI and machine learning expert, will discuss the future of AI, its ethical implications, and how businesses can leverage it for innovation and productivity.

Michael Lee – Founder & CEO of CodeFuture

Michael Lee, founder of CodeFuture, will explore the importance of coding education and how technology can help build a more inclusive and diverse tech workforce.

Samantha Patel – Head of Cybersecurity at SecureNet Solutions

Samantha Patel, a cybersecurity expert, will address current digital threats and provide strategies for organizations to protect their data and infrastructure from cyberattacks.
